Model Airplane Resources

A curated list of my favorite sources for plans, community knowledge, and local Wisconsin aviation hubs.

Primary Plan Sources

Outerzone

The ultimate repository for vintage and old-timer model airplane plans. Thousands of free PDF downloads for builders.

Visit Outerzone

Hip Pocket Aeronautics

A fantastic community and plan gallery focused on free-flight, control-line, and vintage model aviation.

Visit HPA Gallery

AMA Plans Service

The Academy of Model Aeronautics' official plans service, featuring thousands of designs from decades of model aviation history.

Visit AMA Plans

Major RC Communities

Stunthanger

The premier forum for control-line stunt enthusiasts. A wealth of knowledge on building, finishing, and flying.

Visit Stunthanger

RC Groups

The largest online community for radio control and model aviation enthusiasts worldwide.

Visit RC Groups

RC Universe

A massive resource for RC news, reviews, and one of the most active classifieds sections in the hobby.

Visit RC Universe

Control Line & Supplies

Brodak Manufacturing

The leading name in control-line model aviation. Kits, engines, and specialized supplies for the CL flyer.

Visit Brodak

SIG Manufacturing

A legend in the hobby, providing high-quality balsa, kits, and finishing supplies for generations of builders.

Visit SIG

Local Wisconsin Hubs

Circle Masters Flying Club

Wisconsin's premier control-line flying club. Proudly serving the Milwaukee area and beyond.

Visit Circle Masters

Richard Bong State Recreation Area

Primary local site for RC flight, rocketry, and balloons. Features a dedicated Special Use Zone with paved runways.

Park Info

Flying Electrons

Established club in Menomonee Falls. Their Tamarack Airfield is a top-tier facility for planes and helis.

Visit Flying Electrons

Milwaukee RC Association

An umbrella organization for the region, famous for hosting the annual RC Auction & Swap Meet.

Visit MRCA

Racine R/C Club

An active club in Mount Pleasant with a fantastic flying field and a welcoming community.

Visit Racine RC

Hiawatha Hobbies

One of Wisconsin's best full-line hobby stores located in Waukesha for all your modeling needs.

Visit Hiawatha Hobbies

MARKS RC Club

The Milwaukee Area R/K Society, maintaining established flying sites in the region.

Visit MARKS RC

AMA Club Finder

Search the official Academy of Model Aeronautics database for dozens of other clubs across Wisconsin.

Find More Clubs
